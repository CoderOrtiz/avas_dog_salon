Frequently Asked Questions:

How do I cancel an appointment?

In order to cancel an appointment, you will need to call the salon at (813)388-9825. If you need to cancel after hours, you can direct message us on Facebook 24/7, the link is in the top right corner of this page. Please try to give us 24 hours' notice for any cancellations.

Do you reqire any proof of vaccination?

We strongly suggest all pets have their vaccinations but the only one required is rabies. If you do not have the paperwork, proof of a tag and/or your vet's information will work. If you do not have any of these please let the receptionist know and we will work with you.

How long will the grooming service take?

The amount of time it takes for the grooming varies by breed and service, but you can usually plan on it taking 2-3 hours. Express services can be arranged upon request, shortening service time, but may come with an extra cost. Minor services, such as nail trims, take approximately 15 minutes but wait time may apply without an appointment.

When do I need to pick up my dog?

In order to keep noise levels at a minimum and help reduce any anxiety for the dogs we kindly ask you to pick up your pet as soon as possible. Our groomers will give a 10-15 minute heads up to give you extra time to prepare to come to pick up.

What is included in the grooming package?

  • Bath Package Includes Nail Clipping and Grinding, Ear Cleaning and Plucking, Bath with Tearless Face Wash, Shampoo and Conditioner (of owners choice).
  • Mini Groom Package Includes everything in the bath package plus Face, Feet and Fanny Trimming.
  • Full Groom Package includes Nail Clipping and Grinding, Ear Cleaning and Plucking, Bath with Tearless Face Wash, Shampoo and Conditioner (of owners choice) and a Full Body Haircut.
