Marissa Finney

Owner

Marissa has a golden retriever named Ava, and Golden Retrievers are her favorite dogs to groom. She has been in the grooming industry since 18 years old. Her career started as a bather, and after 2 years she was off to grooming school. Marissa has learned the value of patience and compassion. She loves all her clients and their furbabies and treats each one as she would her own.


Georgia Al-Khatib

Groomer

Georgia first began working with dogs at 19 years old as a bather, after a year she made the decision to pursue a career as a groomer and went to grooming school. Now at 27 she has two fur babies Monroe her Dalmatian and Bentley a jackrussel/Maltese mix. She is most passionate about working and helping dogs that can get nervous for grooming and may need some extra love, attention and patience, ensuring that every pet is treated as family and producing beautiful, detailed, quality grooms.


Maegan Daley

Groomer/Bather

Meet Maegan! Our full-time bather/ groomer! Maegan has been grooming for 5 years and has an eye for detail. She is known as the master de-shedder at the salon and she is quite the perfectionist. Maegan prides herself on the quality of her grooms and it definitely shows! She owns a German Shepard named Dogmeat and she is also our Shepard whisperer!
